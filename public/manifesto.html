02

The shift.

AI agents are becoming the primary interface between consumers and brands. A 200-page PDF doesn't negotiate with a shopping assistant. Brand guidelines as we know them cannot survive this shift.

03

Expression containers.

Content is not placed into fixed components. It is expressed through adaptive containers that respond to context in real time. Each container is a decision layer where intent, audience, and situation converge.

SBB example
Brand Color: Locked

#EB0000 appears on every persona's screen. The red zone is always SBB Red. No AI override.

Tone: Controlled

Under 30 = "tu/du." Over 30 business = "Sie/vous/Lei." Rules set by the strategist. AI follows.

Layout: Autonomous

Marco's dashboard has no borders, 16px type, data rows. Isabelle's has 26px type, hero cards, generous padding. The agent decides.

What to notice
  • Language switches: FR, DE, EN, IT
  • Tone: "tu" vs "Sie" vs "Lei"
  • Content: eco for Lena, heritage for Luigi
  • Layout: compact for Marco, generous for Isabelle
  • Products: Seven25 for Sophie, GA for Thomas
Disruption mode

Select Lena, then switch to "Disruption." The entire interface restructures: alert banner, alternative routes, compensation — all proactive. Layer 4 autonomous.
